About Makaha Valley Ranch

We are the Hunter Family, a Canadian transplant to Hawaii who moved to the island three years ago in search of a dream, Hawaii delivered that dream and we settled in a house cradled in Makaha Valley surrounded by lush mountains.

The property features: a full working bar with sink, pool, tiny house with full bathroom/changing area, an outdoor bathroom, outdoor shower, tons of natural plants including fresh coconuts, oranges, variegated lemons (pink lemons), standard and finger limes, papaya, strawberry guava and standard mango.

We can comfortably park approximately 8 cars at the front and up to 10 along the sides and back of the property. The side and back parking is recommended for small cars only as it is a narrow road and rocky.

With over 1/1.5 acres of space there is tons of room to host the event of your dreams. A short walk to Makaha Beach, the property is in an ideal setting for events of any size.
