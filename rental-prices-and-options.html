Rental Prices & Options

Hunter Ranch Makaha is available for rentals EVERY OTHER Friday, Saturday and Sunday. Full dates are available on our calendar page. Reservations are first come first serve and are confirmed once a deposit has been made. We require a 50% deposit 24 hours after booking to confirm your rental. Deposits by check will be confirmed once cleared.

 

All Bronze Rentals include:

  1. Parking for up to 10 cars along the front and sides
  2. Use of the outdoor bar/eating area
  3. Use of the pool 
  4. Use of the entire yard including the bathroom, manmade beach, sunlounging areas etc. within the fence border.

Full Day Rental: $1.000.00 for a group up to 30 people

Half Day Rentals: $500.00 for a group up to 30 people

All Gold Rentals Include:

  1. Parking for up to 10 cars along the front and sides
  2. Use of the outdoor bar/eating area
  3. Use of the pool
  4. Use of the entire yard including the bathroom, manmade beach, sunlounging areas etc. within the fence border.
  5. Use of the tiny house for changing/preping or staging of bridal parties or vip guests
