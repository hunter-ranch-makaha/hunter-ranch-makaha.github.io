Rental Prices & Options

Reservations are first come first serve and are confirmed once a deposit has been made. We require a 50% deposit 24 hours after booking to confirm your rental. Deposits by check will be confirmed once cleared.

All Bronze Rentals include:

  1. Parking for up to 10 cars along the front and sides
  2. Use of the outdoor bar/eating area
  3. Use of the pool 
  4. Use of the entire yard including the bathroom, manmade beach, sunlounging areas etc. within the fence border.

Full Day Rental: $1,000.00 for a group up to 30 people

Half Day Rentals: $500.00 for a group up to 30 people

All Gold Rentals Include:

  1. Parking for up to 10 cars along the front and sides
  2. Use of the outdoor bar/eating area
  3. Use of the pool
  4. Use of the entire yard including the bathroom, manmade beach, sunlounging areas etc. within the fence border.
  5. Use of the tiny house for changing/preping or staging of bridal parties or vip guests

Full Day Rental: $1,800.00 for a group up to 100 people

Half Day Rentals: $900.00 for a group up to 100 people

Parties over 100 people regardless of package type

Full Day Rental: $2,000 for groups 101-150, $2,500 for groups 151-200

Half Day Rentals: $1,200 for groups of 101-150, $1,800 for groups 151-200

MAXIMUM of 250 guests at any time. Guests 201-250 will be charged $75.00 per guest

On the day of the rental, please arrive 15 minutes in advance of your booking time to finalize payment and gain access.
