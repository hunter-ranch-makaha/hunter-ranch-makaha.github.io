Rental Rules and Requirements

Insurance

All rentals must provide PROOF OF INSURANCE prior to deposit.

Eventsured

Special Event Insurance

Rules for All Guests

  1. The main house is off limits to all guests, no guest may enter the home
  2. All guests must clean up after themselves, we have pets that live here, please do not leave food waste on the grass. Bones and other food may be lethal to animals
  3. Garbage must be cleaned and taken to the bins at the front of the house, we are limited to 1 garbage bag per guest, if guests have more than one bag of waste, please take the bags to the dump or pay $75.00 for a dump deposit and have us remove the waste on your behalf
  4. No permanent fixtures, attachments or other alterations may be made on property. Any attachments must be temporary and non damaging.
  5. The tiny house must be left clean, please tidy up and remove any waste upon exit
  6. Please reduce music past 9:00 pm 
  7. No pets allowed
  8. There is NO lifeguard, swim at your own risk.
  9. Do not block the gates, or any access to the house
  10. Do not close the gates without asking in advance
  11. Do not drive on the grass or any of the lawn areas
  12. NO fireworks, sparklers or other explosives

BBQ/Fire Pit rules

  1. Please use the appropriate fuel when lighting any of the bbq/fire pit areas
  2. Ensure you have a bucket of water or sand near by and watch for sparks
  3. Do not light the fire pit if there are high winds or we have informed you it is unsafe to burn at this time

BBQ and grill must be left clean, ashes/coal disposed of approrpiately 

Fruit Picking Guide/Rules

  1. Guests are welcome to pick and use fruit from any of the trees on property as long as they limit themselves to taking no more than 1 piece per person and do not harm or destroy any plants in the process.
  2. We have oranges, pink lemons, limes, finger limes, paypaya, coconut, apple bannanas, avacado, strawberry guava and standard mangos on property.
  3. We are planning a large vegetable garden in the front as well, and we will be offering baskets per guest at a cost of $10.00 a basket for an assorment of vegetables if you wish to take some homegrown items home or use them in eating on site.
